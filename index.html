About Xan

Xan IT Solutions Co., Ltd was established in 2014 as a joint-venture Company of ACE Data Systems Co., Ltd with strong expertise in ebook, e-commerce solutions. Although the company is start-up recently in Myanmar, we have many experiences in developing computer systems for many projects. We are creating Information Technology innovation that is the best suited to meet the requirements of organizations in the public and private sector. We are providing innovative technology and services that help businesses more effective and efficient. Our goal is to develop and innovate the customer-oriented solutions for our valuable clients. Xan IT Solutions Co., Ltd focuses on developing professional solutions based on customer benefits.
We are progressing with strong and skillful developers at open source for a system development. And also providing services in offshore software development, customized software and website development for local organization and we are an authorized distributor of “IP Guard End Point Security System” and “iBeacon wireless transmitter” of foreign IT companies. We are developing “e-Library Management System” for local universities, private schools and colleges. We provide the following services for clients:
  • Digital Book Store
  • E-Library Management System
  • Outsourcing
